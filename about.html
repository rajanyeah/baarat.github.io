, what is it?

Baarat community is a Jaipur based friendly theatre community which focuses on extending the reach and fun of theatre and multiple other creative environments. We host various events and workshops which thrive on freshening up one's mind and encouraging connection with new people. Whether you enjoy writing , film making, acting, designing, or simply exploring Baarat is here to support your journey.
